## How the Game Works
The game picks a secret random number within a range. The player guesses numbers one at a time. After each guess the game says Go Higher, Go Lower, or Correct. The player wins by guessing the exact secret number within the allowed attempts.

## Difficulty Levels
Easy mode uses a range of 1 to 20 and allows 6 attempts. Normal mode uses a range of 1 to 100 and allows 8 attempts. Hard mode uses a range of 1 to 50 and allows 5 attempts.

## Scoring System
Winning scores points based on how quickly you found the answer. Winning early gives more points. The base score for winning is 100 minus 10 times the attempt number. The minimum score for a win is 10 points. Wrong guesses that are too high on even attempts gain 5 points. Wrong guesses that are too low lose 5 points.

## AI Coach
The AI Coach button analyzes your guess history and suggests the best next guess using the Llama 3 language model. It identifies your strategy, recommends a number, and provides a confidence score from 0 to 100 percent.

## AI Chatbot
The AI Chatbot answers questions about strategy, math, and the game rules. It uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to search a knowledge base before answering. You can ask it anything about how to play better.
